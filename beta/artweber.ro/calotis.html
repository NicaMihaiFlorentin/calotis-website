Nentin x Calotis!

Music Bots 8-JAN-2022, 20:38 Mihai



The biggest update, The most stunning version!

Calotis is a feature-rich Discord bot delivering the sweet sound of music from a plethora of websites straight to your server!

Features of Bot

Spotify, Sound Cloud, YouTube, Apple Music, Deezer, Facebook Support
Use your spotify playlist, youtube videos, youtube playlists and much more using this bot.

Lag free music
Bot never lags while playing a song in a voice channel.

Server Settings
Control your song looping song or queue, You can even change server prefix and server dj role.

Commands:

Play Commands:
-play [song] - Play your favorite songs
-pause - Pauses the music
-resume - Resumes the music
-skip - Skip the current song
-loop - Loop the current song
-volume - Check or change the current volume
-nowplaying - See what song is currently playing
-seek - Seek to a position in the song
-bassboost - Enables bass boosting audio effect

Search Commands:
-search [song] - Shows a result of songs based on the search query
-youtube - Starts a YouTube Together session
-lyrics [Song Name] - Shows the lyrics of the song searched

Info Commands:
-stats - Get information about the bot
-help - Information about the bot
-grab - Saves the current song to your Direct Messages

Queue Commands:
-loopqueue - Loop the whole queue
-queue - Shows all currently enqueued songs
-shuffle - Shuffles the queue
-skipto - Skip to a song in the queue
-remove [number] - Remove a song from the queue
-clear - Clears the server queue

Settings Commands:
-config - Edit the bot settings
-disconnect - Stop the music and leave the voice channel
-invite - To invite me to your server
